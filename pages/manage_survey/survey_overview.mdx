# Survey overview

You can access general information about the survey in the Survey Overview tab, which is available in Settings -> Survey Overview or by tapping on the survey name on the main screen.

Here, you can view details such as survey item counts per status, the total number of survey items, and other relevant information.

Additionally, you have the option to change the name of the survey you are currently working on by pressing the "Edit" button next to the survey name.
