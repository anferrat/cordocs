## Bluetooth multimeter

Premium subscription users have the capability to link Bluetooth Low Energy multimeters with the application for the purpose of gathering voltage and current.

Additionally, this feature facilitates the automatic detection and capture of ON and OFF potentials utilizing GPS clock and network time synchronization.
